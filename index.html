Our Story

A Legacy of Cricketing Excellence in Hyderabad

KSM Cricket Academy is built on the principle of nurturing world-class cricketers through innovation, dedication and a culture of giving back to the community. We are one of Hyderabad's leading cricket academies.

With our cutting-edge, technology-enabled curriculum, certified coaches and a world-class ground at Nadargul, Adibatla, we provide elite cricket coaching to national and international cricket aspirants from across Telangana.

Our Programs

Cricket Programs for Every Age

From budding beginners to aspiring professionals, we have a structured pathway designed for every level of cricketer.

5 - 12 yrs

Junior Cricket Program

Fun-based introduction to cricket fundamentals, building hand-eye coordination, balance, basic batting, bowling and fielding skills.

2 hr / session 3 days / week
13 - 15 yrs

Sub-Junior Program

Technique refinement, match awareness, situational drills, fitness training and introduction to the mental side of the game.

2.5 hr / session 5 days / week
16 - 19 yrs

Senior Cricket Program

Match-readiness training, advanced techniques, video analysis, performance tracking and preparation for district / state trials.

3 hr / session 6 days / week

Specialised one-on-one coaching for serious aspirants targeting Ranji, IPL trials and international cricket pathways.
