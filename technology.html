Innovative Tech

Cutting-Edge Player Analytics

KSM uses state-of-the-art technology tools to help every student leverage their performance and improve faster.

01

Video Analysis

High-speed cameras record every session and break down technique frame-by-frame for accurate feedback.

02

Motion Capture

Advanced biomechanical tracking helps detect inefficiencies and prevent injuries through real-time data.

03

AI Performance Insights

Machine learning analyses your batting, bowling and fielding patterns to suggest personalised improvements.

04

Bowling Speed Radar

Track speed, swing and seam movement using professional cricket radar systems used at the international level.

05

Performance Dashboards

Each student has a personalised digital dashboard tracking growth, fitness and match performance over time.

06

Bowling Machines

State-of-the-art bowling machines simulate match conditions, helping batters practice against varied deliveries.

07

Fitness Tracking

Smart wearables track heart rate, recovery and intensity to optimise training load and prevent burnout.

08

Match Simulation

Virtual match scenarios prepare players for high-pressure situations and improve decision-making on the field.
