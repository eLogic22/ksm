Who We Are

Nurturing the Next Generation of Cricketers

KSM Cricket Academy is a trustworthy brand, embodying the spirit of giving back to the cricketing community. We have established ourselves as one of the leading cricket academies in the country, owing to our cutting-edge, technology-enabled curriculum and a wide-reaching network of 60+ centres across India.

Our academy provides high-class cricket coaching, classes and franchise opportunities to both national and international cricket aspirants. Whether you're a beginner or an aspiring professional, we have a program designed for you.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver world-class cricket coaching with a structured curriculum, modern technology and personalised mentorship — making the joy of cricket accessible to every aspiring player in India.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in cricket coaching in India, producing players who not only excel on the field but also become responsible, disciplined and inspiring sportspeople.

Our Values

Integrity, dedication, discipline, innovation and giving back to the community. These principles guide every decision and program at KSM Cricket Academy.
